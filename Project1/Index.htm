Ah, I see you've found me out—yep, I'm Mykull, the man with a furry tail and a brain full of animal secrets. You’re probably wondering about that tail. Well, it's not something that everyone gets, but it's part of the package deal when you’re as connected to the animal kingdom as I am. Sometimes, it twitches when I’m deep in thought (which is a lot). Other times, it’s just there, swishing around in perfect sync with whatever animal is currently communicating with me. I suppose you could call it my “connection antenna.” Now, about the plug in part—I know, that’s a bit weird, but let me explain. You see, my tail’s not just for show. Oh no, it has function. There's this thing I like to call my "Animal Empathy Plug." It's this fancy contraption, kind of like a neural link, that lets me tune into animal minds more clearly. Without it, I'd still be able to hear their thoughts, but with the plug in, I can feel them, too. I know exactly when a cat is feeling smug after knocking over a vase or when a crow is planning its next sarcastic remark about humanity. It's, uh... a lot more intense than people realize. But enough about the tail and the plug—you're probably more curious about what it's like being me, right? Well, let me tell you, it’s a constant adventure. My mornings start with a breakfast of fruit (I’m not eating just for myself, mind you—got to make sure the squirrels approve of the selection). Then, I spend a good portion of the day having conversations with whoever’s around—whether that’s a confused pigeon on my windowsill or a deer who wants to know why humans keep putting up "No Trespassing" signs in the woods. It’s... never boring. Oh, and did I mention the art gallery? Yeah, I got a whole exhibit dedicated to animals making art. The fish in my collection? They create masterpieces with their fins. My personal favorite? A lobster who’s really into minimalism. Don’t ask how I got a lobster to work with oil paints. That’s another story entirely. So yeah, I’ve got a furry tail, a plug in my brain, and a life that’s mostly just filled with animal wisdom, weird art, and lots of animal dreams. It's a weird, wonderful existence, but it’s mine.
